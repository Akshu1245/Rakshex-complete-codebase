- distribute malware, phishing, credential stuffing, denial-of-service traffic, or destructive payloads;
- facilitate unlawful surveillance, discrimination, harassment, fraud, child exploitation, human trafficking, or violent wrongdoing;
- generate or distribute content that is illegal or infringes third-party rights;

We may investigate credible violations, preserve relevant evidence, restrict a feature, suspend access, or terminate under the Terms. Where practicable, we will notify Customer and allow remediation. We may report unlawful conduct when legally required. Report abuse to abuse@rakshex.in.
