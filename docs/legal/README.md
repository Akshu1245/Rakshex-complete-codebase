# RakshEx Legal Package

Version: 2026-07-12

This directory is the source of truth for RakshEx customer-facing legal documents. Generated Word documents are published under `devpulse-frontend/public/legal`.

## Documents

- `TERMS_OF_SERVICE.md`: self-serve and online service terms.
- `PRIVACY_POLICY.md`: privacy notice for account, website, telemetry, and customer-content processing.
- `DATA_PROCESSING_ADDENDUM.md`: controller-to-processor terms and processing annexes.
- `SERVICE_LEVEL_AGREEMENT.md`: enterprise SLA, applicable only when incorporated into an Order Form.
- `ACCEPTABLE_USE_POLICY.md`: prohibited and controlled uses.
- `REFUND_CANCELLATION_POLICY.md`: cancellation, renewal, refund, and billing-error rules.
- `SUBPROCESSOR_REGISTER.md`: production vendor register and change procedure.
- `AI_TRANSPARENCY_STATEMENT.md`: intended use, limits, evidence labels, and human-oversight position.

## Release Rule

These documents are implementation-ready, but they are not a substitute for advice from qualified Indian and international counsel. Before public paid launch, the owner must complete `../operations/LEGAL_LAUNCH_SIGNOFF.md`, including the registered legal entity name, registered address, GST details, named grievance officer, and accepted liability position.

Do not publish a patent, trademark-registration, certification, customer-result, or guaranteed-compliance claim unless the supporting record is attached to the launch sign-off.
