# RakshEx Launch Operating Brief

## Positioning

RakshEx is the AI control plane for teams that need one reliable view of AI credentials, cloud accounts, subscriptions, models, runtime policy, cost, and audit evidence.

Do not position the product as a generic all-in-one AI security platform. That claim is crowded and difficult to prove. The defensible wedge is AI access governance across API keys, cloud identities, and paid developer-tool seats, with runtime enforcement when a customer chooses to route traffic through the gateway.

## Beachhead

Start with platform engineering and application-security teams at companies with 20-300 developers that already use more than one of OpenAI or Anthropic API, Azure OpenAI, Bedrock, GitHub Copilot, Claude Teams, Cursor, or internal agents.

Their first successful workflow should take less than one day:

1. Local or CI scan finds masked AI credentials and SDK usage.
2. Admin records provider accounts and imported subscriptions/seats.
3. Team sees unmanaged usage and ownership gaps.
4. One production integration enforces a budget, provider/model rule, or kill switch.
5. Security lead exports the resulting evidence.

## Launch Assets Required Outside Code

- A named security contact at `security@rakshex.in` and a tested inbox.
- One written security disclosure policy and response SLA.
- Provider OAuth/app registrations approved for each live connector.
- Production payment accounts, tax treatment, invoices, refund policy, and a support owner.
- Customer approval before publishing a logo, quote, benchmark, or savings claim.
- A real status monitor and incident communication process.
- A legal review of privacy policy, terms, DPA, DPDP/GDPR language, and any patent or certification statement.

## Proof Standard

Public marketing may say what the product does and link to a demo, documentation, and audit behavior. It must not claim an external certification, independent audit, performance comparison, customer result, patent grant, or market-first status unless the supporting record is published and current.

## Competitive Response

AI gateways commonly cover routing, rate limits, caching, and observability; AI security products focus on prompt or data protection. RakshEx should win by joining those controls to identity, entitlement, subscription, and cloud-account governance, then labeling evidence quality rather than fabricating precision.

## Metrics That Matter

- Activation: first masked discovery finding, first provider account, first subscription, first policy.
- Expansion: governed providers per workspace, active seats reconciled, gateway-protected requests, audit exports.
- Trust: zero cross-tenant incidents, credential exposure incidents, policy false-block rate, mean time to revoke.
- Revenue: conversion from free scan to team workspace, pilot-to-paid conversion, net revenue retention, sales-cycle time.
