# Privacy Request Runbook

Owner: Privacy Contact

Intake address: privacy@rakshex.in

## Intake and Verification

Log the request date, requester, jurisdiction, request type, account email, workspace, and deadline. Verify identity and authority without collecting more data than needed. For Customer-controlled workspace data, route the request to the Customer administrator unless RakshEx is independently responsible for the processing.

## Classify

Classify as access, correction, deletion, export, objection, restriction, consent withdrawal, marketing opt-out, breach question, or grievance. Check legal hold, security investigation, tax/billing retention, and contractual obligations before deletion.

## Fulfilment

Use product export and deletion controls where available. Confirm scope with the requester, obtain approval from the workspace owner for organisation data, record actions and evidence, and notify subprocessors only where required and feasible. Do not disclose another user's data, provider credentials, raw secrets, or security-sensitive details.

## Closure

Send a plain-language response describing the action, any retained categories and reason, available appeal or complaint path, and contact point. Preserve a minimum request record containing identity-verification outcome, request, decision, action, and dates.

## Escalation

Escalate to counsel for regulator correspondence, requests involving minors, special-category data, law enforcement, cross-border rights, a suspected breach, or a dispute. Track statutory deadlines based on the requester's jurisdiction and the applicable contract.
