# Vendor and Subprocessor Review Checklist

Use before enabling a provider that processes Customer Data or has production access.

- Confirm legal entity, contract, data-processing terms, security contact, and processing regions.
- Identify data categories, purpose, retention, encryption, access controls, and subprocessor chain.
- Review privacy, security, incident notification, deletion, audit, availability, export, and termination terms.
- Confirm minimum privileges, separate credentials, production owner, rotation path, and offboarding steps.
- Check whether the provider is optional, customer-directed, or a RakshEx subprocessor.
- Record an entry in `docs/legal/SUBPROCESSOR_REGISTER.md` before enabling production data.
- Verify system monitoring, support escalation, recovery path, and an alternative or exit plan.
- Obtain Security and Privacy approval for high-risk vendors; involve counsel for regulated or cross-border processing.

Review active material vendors annually and after a material incident or contract change.
