# Legal and Commercial Launch Sign-Off

This checklist must be completed by the business owner and qualified counsel before opening paid sign-up. Do not replace blanks with assumptions.

- [ ] Registered legal entity name, registration number, registered address, and authorised signatory confirmed.
- [ ] GST/tax registration, invoice fields, payment descriptor, refund contact, and jurisdictional tax treatment confirmed.
- [ ] Named Grievance Officer and privacy contact confirmed; role email aliases are tested.
- [ ] Terms, Privacy Policy, Cookie Policy, AUP, Refund Policy, DPA, SLA, and Subprocessor Register reviewed by qualified counsel.
- [ ] Website contact details, legal entity, privacy notice, and billing policy match executed documents.
- [ ] No unverified patent, trademark, certification, customer, benchmark, savings, uptime, or compliance claim remains public.
- [ ] Payment-provider agreements, webhooks, refund flow, chargeback process, and customer support escalation tested.
- [ ] DPDP, IT/SPDI, consumer-law, GDPR, SCC, export-control, and sector-specific obligations assessed for intended markets.
- [ ] Vendor/subprocessor contracts and data-processing terms recorded and current.
- [ ] Incident response, privacy request, retention, deletion, backup restore, and continuity exercises completed and recorded.
- [ ] Insurance, professional advice, and contractual liability caps reviewed for enterprise sales.
